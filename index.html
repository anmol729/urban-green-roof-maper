About Green Roofing

Green roofs are a sustainable building practice that involves growing vegetation on rooftops. They provide numerous benefits, including:

  • Environmental Benefits: Green roofs help reduce urban heat, improve air quality, and manage stormwater runoff.
  • Energy Efficiency: They provide insulation, reducing heating and cooling costs for buildings.
  • Habitat Creation: Green roofs offer habitats for various species, promoting biodiversity in urban areas.
  • Improved Aesthetics: They enhance the visual appeal of buildings and contribute to urban greening.
